No- Show Policy

Definition of a “No-Show” Appointment

defines a “No-show” appointment as any scheduled appointment in which the client either:

– Does not arrive to the appointment

– Cancels with less than 12 hours’ notice

Appointment “No-show” appointments have a significant negative impact on our salon. When a client “no-shows” a scheduled appointment it:

– Is unfair (and frustrating) to other clients that would have taken the appointment slot

– Disrespects not only the technician’s time, but also the time of the entire salon’s staff

How to Avoid Getting a “No-Show”

+ Confirm your appointment

+ Arrive 5-10 minutes early

+ Give 12 hours’ notice to cancel appointment

  1. Appointment Confirmation

Bnails salon will attempt to contact you 1 business days with text message before your scheduled appointment to confirm your visit. If we don’t get a response from the text message, our front desk will try to confirm the appointment by phone the morning of the appointment

  1. Always Arrive 5-10 Minutes Early

we would like for you to arrive at our salon 5-10 minutes prior to your scheduled appointment. This allows time for you and our staff to address any question about trending design/ color/ any color you looking for and trust me, we have 1000+ colors to choose from and this will most likely take you a while to do so before the scheduled time.

  1. Give 12 Hours’ Notice if You Need to Cancel

When you need to cancel or rebook an appointment, please give us at least 12 hours’ notice, this allows us a reasonable amount of time to determine the most appropriate way to reschedule your appointment as well as giving us an opportunity to book someone else for the available spot;

If it is less than 12 hours before your appointment and something comes up, please give us the courtesy of a phone call.

We understand life is busy and things happen all the time, that’s why we have a 4 strikes no-show policy, we will require a credit card information saved on file for any client that had 4 No-show – Any no- show after the 4th times will be charged a fee at $25.
